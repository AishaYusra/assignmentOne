Get to know me!

Aisha-Yusra Shafi'i

My name is Aisha-Yusra Shafi'i. I was born on the 21st of August, 2004 in Kaduna State. I am the last child and the only daughter. I attended Mary Travis School for my Primary Education between 2008-2015, after which I proceeded to Zamani College for my Secondary Education from 2015-2021, all in Kaduna State. I am currently a 2nd year student aiming for a Bachelors of Science in Computer Science at Nile University of Nigeria, Abuja. I enjoy baking, trying out new recipes, reading novels sometimes, as well as watching football, movies and series particularly Political Thrillers, Crime shows, Romance, Sit-coms & Psychological Thrillers. I would describe myself as an easy-going and fun person even though i don't really like to do much. I'm pretty ambiverted as well as i'm not always in the mood to be hyper, sometimes i enjoy the calm and quiet.
To give you a bit of an insight in my choice of course of study, since i was much younger, i particularly saw myself being an Architect or Interior Designer ...Computer Science was never on my radar up until I wrote my JAMB exams because Architecture was still my first choice which my school made me change since i wasn't studying it afterall. After a lot of research, thoughts and consultations, i started exploring other options within the Pure Sciences and did some research on the tech industry to which i really liked a lot though a lot of things looked quite challenging. I discovered that studying Computer Science would cover all the facets of the tech industry, that way i can try different things instead of just rushing into something. So far, my journey has been kind of okay but there has been a few ups and downs. But it does not mean i have given up on my Interior Designing dream. I still plan on expanding my knowledge! So, in the future i plan on incorporating the skills i acquire in my tech journey into my love for Interior Design.
